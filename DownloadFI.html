PLP Fuelinfo

What is PLP-Fuelinfo?

PLP-Fuelinfo is an official, licensed, modernly designed and easy-to-use application from ProgrammerLP to help you keep track of and manage your fuel expenses. Please note the PLP non-open source license!

Why PLP-Fuelinfo?

Beautiful Design

PLP-Fuelinfo offers you a wonderful, modern and a simple design, with it comes a simple but a very good darkmode, shortcuts to navigate to certain features, a responsive UI and much more, which simplifies the usage. Try it for yourself.

View your Statistics

PLP-Fuelinfo statistics provides you with a lot of useful information about your fuel consumption. That's why the statistics goes over 2 pages, e.g. how much you spend on what, how much you spend on fuel, how much you paid per liter, average spending on fuel and more. Try it out for yourself.

Many Categories

PLP-Fuelinfo offers you a beautiful design as well as many useful categories to manage your entries, of course these categories can also be found in the statistics. Try it yourself
