PLP Finance

What is PLP-Finance?

PLP-Finance App is an official, licensed, modernly designed and easy-to-use application from ProgrammerLP, so you never lose track of your finances. Please note the PLP non-open source license!

Why PLP-Finance?

Beautiful Design

PLP-Finace offers you a wonderful, modern and a simple design, with it comes a simple but a very good darkmode, many shortcuts to navigate to certain features, a responsive UI and much more, which simplifies the usage. Try it for yourself.

View your Statistics

PLP-Finance statistics provides you with a lot of useful information about your finances, e.g. how much you spend on what, how much you earn from what, average income and expenses, total income and expenses and more. Try it for yourself.

Altenative Listview

PLP-Finance also offers you, besides the beautiful design, the possibility to switch between two display modes, so you can choose the card view or the list view. Try it yourself
