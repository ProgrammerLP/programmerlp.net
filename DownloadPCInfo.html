PC Info

What is PC-Info?

PLP PC-Info is an official, free open-source, modernly designed and easy to use program from ProgrammerLP, so you can find out what components are installed in your PC and how your PC is utilized.

Why PC Info?

Many useful information

PC-Info provides you with a variety of useful information about your PC, including system information, such as operating system, ram, processor, graphics card and more. Try it for yourself.

View your Drives

PC-Info offers you besides the system information also the possibility to give you information about your drives, including the memory usage, the total capacity and more. Try it yourself.

Beautiful Darkmode

PC-Info offers you a very good darkmode, which is activated by default, moreover the whole UI adapts to the darkmode, so that you are not limited in its use. We recommend you to test the dark mode in any case.
